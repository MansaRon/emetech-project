VOIP PHONE SYSTEMS

Our VoIP telephones and systems offer advanced, future-proof communication solutions at a much lower cost to traditional phone systems. We cover all aspects of VoIP telephone systems, including connectivity, SIP trunks, installation, hardware, training and support.

BENEFITS OF VOIP

Removing fixed telephone lines and replacing them with cheaper SIP trunks, significantly reduces call costs compared to traditional telecoms. Because of this, companies moving to VoIP telephones can save as much as 40% over their existing telecoms solution. While companies may be concerned about the outlay for replacing phone hardware, they needn’t be. VoIP phone software allows you to access your telephone system and make calls from your PC with a headset Additionally, you can remove the need for a desk phone altogether with the 3CX mobile app.

Should your organisation still want desk phones however, Emetech Solution can supply phone hardware at a range of price points. Alternatively, we can offer a leasing option to help spread the initial cost of switching to a VoIP telephone system.
